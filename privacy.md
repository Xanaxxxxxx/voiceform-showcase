# Voiceform Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 30, 2026

Voiceform is a real-time voice feedback app. This policy explains what information the app uses and how it is handled.

## Information We Collect

Voiceform uses the microphone only when you start a voice practice or feedback session. Microphone audio is used to provide live voice feedback such as pitch, resonance, and thinness indicators.

The app may store practice history, session summaries, and related settings on your device so you can review your progress.

## On-Device Processing

Voiceform is designed as a local-first app. Voice analysis for the core practice experience runs on the device. We do not sell your personal data.

## Microphone Access

Microphone permission is required for live voice feedback. You can revoke microphone access at any time in iOS Settings.

## Data Sharing

Voiceform does not sell user data. Data may be shared only when you choose to export or share it through system-provided iOS sharing features.

## Diagnostics

During beta testing, Apple TestFlight may provide crash reports, diagnostics, and tester feedback through Apple's systems. These reports help us improve reliability and usability.

## Children

Voiceform is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can help address it.

## Changes

We may update this policy as Voiceform evolves. Updates will be posted on this page.
